Suppliers(sid, sname, address)
Parts(pid, pname, colour)
Catalog(supplier, part, cost)

Find the names of suppliers who supply some red part.

RedParts = Sel[colour = 'red'](Parts)
RedPartsCatalog = RedParts Join[pid=part] Catalog
RedPartsSuppliers = RedPartsCatalog Join[supplier=sid] Suppliers
Output = Proj[sname](RedPartsSuppliers)

Find the sids of suppliers who supply some red or green part.

RedParts = Sel[colour = 'red'](Parts)
GreenParts = Sel[Colour = 'green](Parts)
RedOrGreenParts = RedParts Union GreenParts
PartsCatalog = RedOrGreenParts Join[pid=part] Catalog
PartsSuppliers = PartsCatalog Join[supplier=sid] Suppliers
Output = Proj[sname](PartsSuppliers)
